# Hazards

Hazards are the non-combat dangers characters face in the world. Careless adventurers die to these as often as to monsters.

- [Damage](Damage.md) — wound thresholds, death, and being incapacitated

- [Physical Deterioration](Physical%20Deterioration.md) — starvation, thirst, exhaustion, and exposure over time

- [Substance and Toxins](Substance%20and%20Toxins.md) — poisons, venoms, alcohol, and alchemical substances

- [Environmental](Environmental.md) — fire, falling, drowning, extreme cold and heat
