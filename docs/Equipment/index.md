# Equipment

All equipment a character may purchase or acquire during play. Items are purchased during [character creation](../Character/Starting%20Possessions.md) or between expeditions.

> *Italicized* items are **Non-Encumbering** for encumbrance purposes, though the Referee may rule that large quantities count. Items in ***bold italics*** are **Oversized** and impose additional encumbrance penalties.

## Categories

- [Armor](Armor.md) — protective gear from padded cloth to plate armour

- [Weapons](Weapons/index.md) — melee, ranged, and firearms

- [Animals](Animals.md) — mounts, pack animals, and beasts of burden

- [Containers](Containers.md) — bags, chests, and carrying equipment

- [Food](Food.md) — rations, provisions, and consumables

- [Lodging](Lodging.md) — accommodation costs and inn prices

- [Vehicles](Vehicles.md) — carts, wagons, boats, and other conveyances

- [Services](Services.md) — hireable services available in settlements

- [Miscellaneous](Miscellaneous.md) — tools, torches, rope, and general adventuring gear
