# Weapons

All weapons available for purchase. See the [Equipment](../index.md) page for encumbrance rules.

> **Note:** Weapon Skill (WS) applies to melee attacks; Ballistic Skill (BS) applies to ranged and firearm attacks. Some weapons have special rules noted in their entries.

## Categories

- [Melee Weapons](Melee%20Weapons.md) — swords, axes, spears, and other hand-to-hand arms

- [Ranged Weapons](Ranged%20Weapons.md) — bows, crossbows, and thrown weapons

- [Firearms](Firearms.md) — black-powder pistols and long arms

> **Firearms note:** Firearms are powerful but slow to reload and unreliable in wet conditions. They ignore most armour but require careful maintenance.
