# Cleric

## Careers

- [Priest](Careers/Priest.md) — ordained minister of a faith, serving a congregation and deity

## Special Rules

Clerics operate under divine rules distinct from arcane magic:

- [Miracles](Special%20Rules/Miracles.md) — how divine miracles are granted and used

- [Holy Activities](Special%20Rules/Holy%20Activities.md) — religious duties and their mechanical effects

- [Blessings](Special%20Rules/Blessings.md) — the forms of divine favour a Cleric can bestow

- [Holy Water](Special%20Rules/Holy%20Water.md) — consecration and use of holy water

> **Important:** Clerics must remain faithful to their deity's alignment. Acting against it risks losing access to miracles.
