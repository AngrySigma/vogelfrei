# Priest

| Level | New traits      | 1st | 2nd | 3rd |
| ----- | --------------- | --- | --- | --- |

The numbered columns show how many miracles of each spell level the Priest may keep memorized at one time. A Priest receives no miracles at 1st level; until the Invoke Talent is gained at 2nd level, divine power is expressed only through [Blessings](../Special%20Rules/Blessings.md). See [Miracles](../Special%20Rules/Miracles.md) for how miracles are prepared and invoked.
