# Warrior

## Careers

Warriors are the most career-diverse class. Choose or roll for one of the following:

- [Duellist](Careers/Duellist.md) — a fencer and point-of-honour specialist

- [Knight](Careers/Knight.md) — armoured nobility sworn to a lord or cause

- [Mercenary](Careers/Mercenary.md) — a sword for hire, experienced in organized warfare

- [Pit Fighter](Careers/Pit%20Fighter.md) — a hardened brawler from the fighting pits

- [Soldier](Careers/Soldier.md) — disciplined infantry from a standing army

- [Witch Hunter](Careers/Witch%20Hunter.md) — zealous agent hunting heretics and the supernatural
