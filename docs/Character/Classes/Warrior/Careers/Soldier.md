# Soldier

**Class**: [Warrior](../Warrior.md)

**Status**: Brass

**Combat Skills**: WS

!!! tip "Trait"
    Soldiers reduce their Encumbrance by 1 and suffer only half damage from [forced marches](../../../../Adventuring/Time%20and%20Movement.md). Soldiers require 1 hour less sleep than normal.
